## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A valid email address
- Government-issued ID for verification (passport or driver's license)
- A cryptocurrency wallet or bank account for funding
- Two-factor authentication app (e.g., Google Authenticator)

<Callout kind="info">
  SystemForge requires KYC verification to comply with regulations. The process takes under 5 minutes.
</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to sign up and get verified.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user">
    Visit `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`.

    Enter your email, create a strong password, and agree to terms.

    Check your inbox for the verification email and click the link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete KYC" icon="shield">
    Navigate to the Verification section in your profile.

    Upload your ID photo and a selfie.

    Submit and wait for approval (usually instant).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA" icon="lock">
    Go to Security settings.

    Scan the QR code with your authenticator app and enter the code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fund Your Trading Wallet

Deposit funds using one of these methods. Minimum deposit is `{100}` USD equivalent.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Crypto Deposit" icon="dollar-sign">
    Copy your unique wallet address from the Wallet page.

    Send BTC, ETH, or USDT from your external wallet.

````bash
Wallet Address: bc1qxy2kgdygjrsqtzq2n0yrf2493p83kkfjhx0wlh  // Example BTC address
Network: Bitcoin Mainnet
Minimum: 0.001 BTC
````

    Confirmations appear in your dashboard within 10-30 minutes.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bank Transfer" icon="credit-card">
    Select ACH or Wire Transfer in the Funding section.

    Copy the bank details and initiate a transfer from your bank.

    Reference your SystemForge account ID in the memo.

    Funds arrive in 1-3 business days.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Start with a small deposit like `{50}` USD to test the platform.
</Callout>

## Access the Dashboard

1. Log in at `https://dashboard.example.com`.
2. Use your email, password, and 2FA code.
3. Review your balance in the Wallet tab.
4. Explore the SF Matrix indicator on the Markets page for trade signals.

## Place Your First Manual Trade

Execute a simple BTC/USD spot trade.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Market" icon="trending-up">
    Go to Markets > BTC/USD.

    Review the SF Matrix signals and current price (e.g., `{60,500}` USD).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Order" icon="plus">
    Click "Trade".

    Choose Buy, amount `{0.001}` BTC, market or limit order.

    Review fees (`{0.1%}` maker/taker).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and Execute" icon="check-circle">
    Enter 2FA code.

    Submit. Monitor in the Open Orders tab.
  </Step>
</Steps>
