## Overview

The SF Matrix algorithm powers SystemForge's automated trading. It analyzes market signals to open and close positions.

## How to Activate

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to the "Algorithms" section
3. Select "SF Matrix"
4. Click "Activate" and confirm your trading parameters

## Where to See Results

- Monitor trades on the "Dashboard"
- View trade logs in the "History" tab

<Callout kind="info">
Results update every minute during market hours.
</Callout>
