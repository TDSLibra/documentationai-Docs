---
title: Order Not Executing
description: Fix unexecuted trade orders in SystemForge.
---

## Symptoms

- Placed orders stay in "pending"
- No trade confirmation

## Possible Causes

- Insufficient account balance
- Exchange API issue
- Invalid trading pair selected

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check balance">
    ````bash
    # Log in and verify your available cryptocurrency funds
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify trading pair">
    Ensure pair is supported by SystemForge and your exchange.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect API">
    Visit "Settings" > "Exchange API" and reauthorize connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
